Virasat 2022

VIRASAT is the official cultural extravaganza organized by SPIC MACAY NIT Durgapur Chapter. The perfect blend of gorgeous grace with sonorous tune swirls in the flavour of Indian culture and tradition. Esteemed artists of the likes of Pt Viswa Mohan Bhatt(Grammy Award Winner), Dr. N. Rajam, Pt. Ajoy Chakraborty etc. have been show stealers in the recent past. So what are you waiting for? Fasten your seatbelts and get ready to soak in India's rich heritage at its finest.




Guests

  • Dr. Rajeeb Chakraborty
    The name Dr. Rajeeb Chakraborty breathes an astounding versatility of self-expression as a musician, a music composer, and conductor. Along with being a celebrated Sarod Maestro, Dr. Chakraborty has a separate identity as an instructor of Indian classical music in premier universities in India and overseas as well, including Harvard, Carnegie Mellon, and Manchester. Achievements include his degree in Western classical music from the Royal College of Music (London) with distinction. The scholar also received numerous awards for his talent, showcased in countless concerts and major festivals across the western world.
  • Kalam Patua
    Born into the Patua community of scroll painters and storytellers, Kalam Patua is the first patachitra artist who revived the Kalighat style of painting in the modern idiom. Recognition and appreciation came his way for his art contributions to numerous national and international exhibitions. His prolific modern artworks further won him the Aditya Vikram Birla Kala Kiran Puraskar(2003-04), and are amongst the collections of the National Gallery of Modern Art, Delhi, The Victoria Albert Museum, London, and The Museum of Sacred Arts, Belgium, and in several private collections in India and abroad.
